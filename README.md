## Instructions

* PUSH shirukens away by clicking or touching the area of the screen that it's coming from.
* PULL extra lives towards you by clicking on the ninja in the center of the screen.
* Each shiruken pushed offscreen earns you 1 point.
* Each extra life earns you 5 points, or 20 points if you already have full life.

* The game works with a mouse or touchscreen.
* Works best in Firefox.
